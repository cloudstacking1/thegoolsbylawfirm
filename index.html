Be Aware of the Drug Possession Charges in Texas

Drug possession laws in Texas are strict, and penalties can vary depending on the type and amount of drugs found in your possession. Under the Texas Controlled Substances Act drugs are classified into different penalty groups: Penalty Group 1 Includes cocaine, heroin, methamphetamine, and fentanyl. Penalty Group 2 Includes ecstasy, PCP, and certain hallucinogens. Penalty Group 3 & 4 Includes prescription drugs such as Valium and Xanax when possessed without a valid prescription. Possessing even a small amount of drugs related substance can result in significant legal penalties.
